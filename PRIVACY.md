# PriceWatch Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 14, 2026**

## Overview
PriceWatch is a browser extension that helps users save products, compare prices, and track price history. Your privacy is our top priority.

## Data Collection
PriceWatch does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All product data (titles, prices, images, tags, notes) is stored **locally in your browser** using Chrome's built-in storage API.

## What We Store Locally
- Saved product information (title, price, image URL, store name)
- Price history for saved products
- User preferences (dark mode, tags, notes)
- License key (for Pro users)

## What We Do NOT Do
- We do NOT send any data to external servers
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT use cookies or analytics
- We do NOT collect personal information
- We do NOT sell or share any data

## Third-Party Services
- **PayPal**: If you purchase PriceWatch Pro, payment is processed by PayPal on their website. PriceWatch does not handle or see your payment details.

## Data Deletion
You can delete all saved data at any time through the extension's Settings page, or by uninstalling the extension.
